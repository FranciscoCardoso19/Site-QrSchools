Our App

The QRSchools project aims to develop a web application and one for mobile devices that allow optimizing the management and organization of technological equipment in schools.

"A practical and efficient tool to simplify the management of technological equipment in schools, all in one place!"

Enter a new era of equipment management

Our project aims to help schools improve the management of their technological resources by providing a solution that simplifies the work of those responsible for equipment maintenance and control. By optimizing organization, the time spent on administrative tasks is reduced, and the risks of equipment loss or damage are minimized.
